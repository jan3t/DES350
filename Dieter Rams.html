Dieter Rams'

10 Principles
of Good
Design

1

A good design is aesthetic.

Aesthetics in design psychologically contributes to the well-being of people’s everyday lives. It is not only based on beauty, but how one can manipulate the appearance to reflect on the design’s purpose overall.
2
A good design is unique.
A good design typically stands out among other designs due to having an uncommon feature. Establishing a style that is uncommonly used or seen can be successful if the discipline of design is still applicable.
